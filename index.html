Taylor 101

Strategy Values Fun Facts

  • Customer Driven
  • Align ourselves with leaders in each target market segment
  • Consistently deliver high quality products and excellent service.
  • Install systems, products and processes that help key customer partners grow and enhance their business.
  • Utilize size and vertical integration to be low cost / high value supplier.
  • Provide a work environment that attracts and retains partners who are capable, committed and empowered to make a positive impact.

  • Passion
  • Honesty/Integrity
  • Trusting/Trustworthy
  • Respect
  • Teamwork

  • We make 1 in 3 salads consumed in the United States.
  • Our products are enjoyed by 120 million Americans every week
  • We are North America's largest supplier of value-added healthy fresh foods.
  • We have 14 operating companies located across North America.
